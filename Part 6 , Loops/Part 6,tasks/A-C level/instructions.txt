 Play again system
 Take your Lesson 5 game and add "play again" functionality:
Put your entire game inside a loop
After game ends, ask "Play again? (yes/no)"
If yes, play again
If no, exit
Example:
text
You are in a dark room...
[game plays]
Play again? (yes/no): yes
You are in a dark room...
[game plays again]
Play again? (yes/no): no
Thanks for playing!
