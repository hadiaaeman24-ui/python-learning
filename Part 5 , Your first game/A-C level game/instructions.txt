Three-choice adventure
Make a game that:
Describes a situation
Gives 3 different choices
Each choice leads to a different outcome
Use elif to handle all choices
example
A treasure hunt
What do you do?
1. Open the chest
2. Leave it alone
3. Kick it
Your choice: 1
You open the chest! Inside is gold and jewels. You are rich!
Your choice: 2
You are poor
Your choice: 3
You fell down


Two-step adventure
First choice leads to a second situation
Second choice leads to final outcome
Use nested if (if inside if)
You are in a forest. You see a path and a river.
Which way? (path/river): path
You follow the path. You see a cave and a tree.
Enter cave or climb tree? (cave/tree): cave
You enter the cave. You find a sleeping bear! You run away!

exampel 
choice1 = input("First choice: ")
if choice1 == "path":
    choice2 = input("Second choice: ")
    if choice2 == "cave":
        print("Cave ending")
    elif choice2 == "tree":
        print("Tree ending")
elif choice1 == "river":
    choice2 = input("Other choices: ")
