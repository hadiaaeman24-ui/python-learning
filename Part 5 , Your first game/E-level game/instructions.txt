Simple choice game
Shows the player a situation
Gives 2 choices
Reacts differently based on what they choose
example
You are in a dark room. There is a door and a window.
What do you do?
1. Open the door
2. Look out the window
Your choice: 1
You open the door. A friendly cat greets you! You win!
your choice = 2
You saw a ghost and you are injured.
